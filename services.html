An All-Inclusive Package

Life Is More Beautiful When You Get More Bang For Your Buck

As a freelance web developer, I specialize in providing a range of essential services tailored specifically to small businesses. With my expertise, I offer the following services to help enhance your online presence and drive business growth:

Custom, Hand-coded Website I create unique and visually appealing websites that are built from scratch, tailored to your business requirements. By employing hand-coding techniques, I ensure that your website is optimized for performance, responsiveness, and user experience, resulting in a professional online presence that stands out from the competition.

Internal SEO Optimization I understand the importance of search engine visibility in driving organic traffic to your website. With my expertise in internal Search Engine Optimization (SEO), I optimize the structure, content, and meta-tags of your website to improve its search engine rankings. This means your business can be easily found by potential customers searching for your products or services.

24/7 Content Management Keeping your website up-to-date with fresh content is vital for engaging visitors and maintaining relevance. I offer round-the-clock content management services, ensuring that your website remains current and compelling. Whether you need regular updates, blog posts, or news articles, I'm here to manage your content efficiently and promptly.

Research and Copywriting Crafting compelling and persuasive copy is crucial for conveying your brand's message and attracting customers. I provide comprehensive research and copywriting services to create engaging content that captivates your audience and drives conversions. From website copy to blog posts and promotional materials, I can help you effectively communicate your unique value proposition.
